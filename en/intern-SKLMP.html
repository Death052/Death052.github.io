SKLMP Internship

Role: Website Programmer and Designer

Period: June 2023 - Aug 2023

About the Internship

I used Wix to design and program the SKLMP website. I was responsible for planning and executing website designs requested by my supervisors. I produced wireframe diagrams before implementing the actual webiste designs. Even though Wix can be used without any programming skills, I written some javascript functions for additional functionalities. The website is used for displaying SKLMP programmes and events.

About the Company

SKLMP (now known as SKLMEH -- State Key Laboratory of Marine Environmental Health) is a research institution branch managed by the Department of Chemistry at City University of Hong Kong, which focuses on ocean and marine related research.
