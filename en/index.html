About Me

Hi! I am Stephen Kwok Yik Hei, both a game designer and a coder. I have a huge passion for designing games and have high standards for my work. I have been involved in countless game projects, working with different teams and taking up different roles.

Currently, I am working on a metroidvania game called "Within the Trenches" with a team of 2 other people, where I am focused on narrative, art and scripting. Before that, I have worked on solo projects and game jam projects. I have also some internship experiences in RTHK and SKLMP, taking up various techinical and design roles. I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow as a game designer.

I have 3 years of experience in using various game engines, including Unity and Godot, but I am also learning to use Unreal Engine for my final year project in Goldsmiths University. I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow as a game designer.
