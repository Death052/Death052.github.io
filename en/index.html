About Me

Hi! I am Stephen Kwok Yik Hei, specializing in programmer andgame technical design. I created games and game assets for both personal and collaborative projects, but I particularly enjoy working within multidisciplinary teams.

I have 3 years of hands-on development experience developing games in Unity and Godot, while expanding my skills in Unreal Engine. In addition to programming, I'm comfortable creating game assets using various tools both in 2D and 3D fronts.

With a BSc in Computer Science (University of Leeds) and a MA in Games and Playful Design (Goldsmiths), I sit at the intersection of technology and design. I specialize in bridging creative vision with technical implementation, turning raw gameplay concepts into polished player experiences.
